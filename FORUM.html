Conclusion
In the context of the deepening integration of AI and society, it is significant to start rethinking social relations and preparing for the "smart" world of the future. It can be determined that in the age of massive data stacks, AI applications can better estimate solutions to problems, just as in medicine, where artificial algorithms can help to reduce the likelihood of human error in critical tasks in the face of massive amounts of data, or in the economy, where humans can reap more favorable benefits, by optimizing cooperation between humans and AI. In short, instead of fearing AI technology, people should build further equal, just, and happy civilized societies through the use of AI.
